Our Story 💚

🌸 The First Conversation
25 Sept

We were in the same batch for so long… attending the same classes, being around the same people… but somehow, we never really talked.
It’s strange how two people can be so close physically and still feel like strangers.
Then fest happened. You joined our music band as a singer, and that’s when we had our first real conversation.
It wasn’t anything dramatic… just normal talking, normal interaction.
But looking back now, that small moment feels so important. Because that’s where everything started—quietly, without either of us realizing what it would turn into.

🏸 Coincidence… or Something More?
10 Oct

I was just randomly asking people to be my badminton partner… no expectations, no planning. And you were the first one to say yes—almost instantly. It wasn’t like you were super into the sport or anything, but you still showed up, stayed there, played with me, and most importantly… supported me.
You came to watch my matches, cheered for me, stood there like it actually mattered. And I remember feeling genuinely happy seeing you there. At that time, I brushed it off as just coincidence.
But now it feels like one of those moments that was meant to happen.

🎧 When We Actually Started Talking
12 Oct

Around the sports days, something shifted.
We didn’t just know each other anymore—we started talking. Properly. Naturally. Conversations didn’t feel forced or new.
There was no awkwardness, no hesitation… it just flowed. And the weird part was—it felt familiar. Like I had known you for much longer than I actually had.
That’s when talking to you stopped being just “normal” and started becoming something I looked forward to every day.

🖤 All Black Night
17 Oct

That night is still one of the clearest memories I have.
Everything was happening at once—music, chaos, lights, people… but somehow, all I remember is you. We were both dressed in black, and you looked unreal.
There were so many photos, so many small interactions… but what stayed with me was how we were just together the whole time.
Giving you my black shacket because it was cold felt like such a small thing… but it felt right. Natural. Somewhere between all that noise and excitement, something quietly changed inside me.
I didn’t say it then… but I felt it.

💃 Blue Saree Night
17 Oct

And then you came in that blue saree… I don’t think I’ve ever seen someone look that beautiful. Not just because of what you were wearing, but because of how effortlessly you carried yourself.
We spent hours together that night—talking, laughing, just being around each other. Time felt weirdly fast… like everything was happening too quickly but still perfectly.
That night didn’t feel like just another event. It felt like something I’d remember for a long time.

🔥 Fashion Show & DJ Night
18 Oct

That day was chaos… but the good kind. You in that white-pink outfit… and then later in red… honestly, I didn’t even know what to say.
I just kept noticing you again and again. And the funny part? We kept matching without even planning it.
We roamed around the whole night with everyone—playing, laughing, doing random things… but somehow, we always ended up together. Even in a crowd, it felt like it was just you and me.

🎭 That One Night We Got Paired
18 Oct

Out of everyone there, we somehow ended up paired together. And that small thing changed everything a little. We talked… properly. Not just surface-level conversations, but the kind where you start understanding each other.
That night felt different. Not loud or chaotic—just meaningful in a quiet way. I didn’t say it out loud… but I knew something was building.

🏸 After the Break, It Became Us
4 Nov

After the break, things didn’t go back to how they were before.
They became something else. We started spending time together almost every day—badminton, walking, random conversations, constant texting.
It slowly turned into “us” in everything. I kept telling myself you were just friendly… just extroverted… but deep down, I knew it didn’t feel normal anymore.

🧠 The Realization
18 Nov

I started noticing the little things. The way you reacted to certain situations. The way you cared. The way you got a little jealous sometimes. I won’t lie—I even created situations just to understand what you felt. And slowly, everything started making sense. There was something real between us… and I knew it.

❤️ The Confession
29 Nov

You were the one who said it first.
Maybe you weren’t completely sure in that moment… but you still chose to say it. And that’s what made it special.
It wasn’t dramatic. It wasn’t perfectly planned. It was just real. And it meant everything.

💞 The Beginning of Us
30 Nov

This is where everything became official—us.
No more guessing, no more confusion. Just you and me. And the surprising part was… it didn’t feel overwhelming.
It felt calm. Like this was exactly where I was meant to be.

🤝 First Hand Hold
1 Dec

That moment was simple… but it changed everything.
You asked so innocently, and when our hands finally met… it felt different. Your hands were cold, mine were warm… but somehow they fit perfectly.
Walking like that around campus, quietly hoping no one notices… felt like we had our own little world.

🤍 First Hug
1 Dec

That hug… I still remember it clearly.
It wasn’t planned, it just happened.
For a moment, everything went quiet. And in that silence, I realized how much I liked being close to you.
It felt safe. Warm. Real.

🎧 Our First Little Chapter
4 Dec

Our first real “date”… even though we never actually called it that.
I had my badminton tournament, but somehow, bringing you along made it feel completely different.
You weren’t just there—you were with me.
Watching my matches with that excitement, supporting me like it actually mattered… it meant more than I showed. And then the little moments—playing badminton on the streets at night, laughing over nothing, trying to copy those random reels… Walking hand in hand under dim lights, like the world had slowed down just for us.
It wasn’t anything grand.
But somehow, it became one of the most special beginnings.

🚌 That Bus Ride
4 Dec

The bus was almost empty that night.
Quiet, slow, peaceful.
We sat together… not saying much, but not needing to either.
You hugged me, and we held hands the whole way back. It was such a small moment… something anyone else would forget. But for me, it felt perfect.
Like time paused just long enough for me to realize how much I liked being there with you.

🍜 Sharing Everything
5 Dec

The next day felt just as simple… and just as special.
You were there again, watching my match like my own personal cheerleader.
And then we shared food—chicken noodles, pasta… feeding each other, laughing like it was nothing.
But those small moments didn’t feel small.
They felt like the kind of memories that quietly become important later.
That’s when I realized… I wanted more days like this, with you.

🌙 The Quiet Evening
6 Dec

That evening felt different from everything else.
We walked into that usually crowded place… but it was completely silent. Almost like it was waiting for us.
The guard teasing us, telling us to stay away from cameras… and both of us blushing for no reason—it felt so random, yet so real.
We sat there, talking softly, holding hands, laughing quietly.
It felt peaceful.
Like the world outside didn’t exist for a while.
Just you, me, and that moment.

💋 Our First Kiss
6 Dec

It wasn’t planned.
It wasn’t something we talked about.
It just… happened.
We were already close, already holding each other… and when we looked at each other, something shifted.
And then, our first kiss.
Soft. Warm. Gentle.
It felt unreal in the simplest way possible.
Like time slowed down just enough for us to feel everything.

💞 The Day After
7 Dec

The next day felt different.
Not awkward, not strange… just deeper.
Like something had changed between us, and we both knew it.
We met again, sat together, talked, stayed close…
It wasn’t just excitement anymore.
It felt like we were slowly becoming something real.

🏠 A Day Just for Us

That day wasn’t about going out or doing something big.
It was just about being together.
No distractions, no rush… just time.
Staying close, talking, laughing, existing in the same space.
Cuddling, kissing, hugging, into each other.
It felt calm.
Like being with you was enough.

And then dinner, and that cab ride back…
Holding you close, not wanting the night to end.

it’s not the place or the plan…

🩺 Even From Far, Still With Me

These days weren’t easy…
Exams, distance, everything happening at once.
And then my surgery…
But even when you weren’t physically there, you were still with me.
Through calls, messages, check-ins… every little thing.
And then today—our 4 months.
What you made for me wasn’t just a gift.
It was meaning.
The chick… you being with me.
The octopus… keeping me strong.
The football… reminding me to come back stronger.
The donut… your cute way of caring.
The flower… hope that doesn’t fade.
I don’t think I’ll ever forget this.
Not just because it’s our 4 months…
but because it showed me how much you care.
